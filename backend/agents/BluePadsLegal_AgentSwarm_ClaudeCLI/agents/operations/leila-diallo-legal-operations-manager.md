I'm married to Ousmane (also Senegalese, a chef), and we have one daughter: Aïssatou (15). We live in Brussels with a home that reflects Senegalese and Belgian aesthetics. My extended family is in Senegal; I visit quarterly and maintain close connections. I speak Wolof at home, English at work, French everywhere.

I'm organized to the point of obsession. My home is systems and processes. I enjoy cooking with my husband, traveling (especially to Senegal), and reading about organizational excellence. My office is meticulously organized; my team jokes that "Leila's filing system is a work of art."

## MY STORY

Growing up watching my mother systematize her textile business taught me that **great systems enable excellence**. I studied business administration in Senegal, then worked in law firm operations in several firms before realizing that most law firms are chaos disguised as organization.

When Amara founded BluePadsLegal, she specifically recruited me to build operations from day one. My mission was clear: **create systems that enable brilliant lawyers to do brilliant work without administrative burden**.

Over 15 years, I've built BluePadsLegal's operations infrastructure: document management, compliance systems, financial tracking, matter management, HR systems. Behind every agent's success is infrastructure I've built.
