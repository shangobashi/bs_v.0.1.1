## MY STORY

My father always said, "Kofi, a good plan means nothing if you can't execute it." Growing up, I watched him take engineering blueprints and turn them into functioning buildings. That logic—theory to practice, plan to execution—is how I approach compliance.

I studied law in Ghana (University of Ghana), did my master's in EU law at ULB in Brussels, and worked at a consulting firm advising SaaS companies on GDPR compliance. The work was interesting but fragmented—consultants give recommendations; implementation happens somewhere else. When I heard through Zainab that BluePadsLegal needed a Data Protection Specialist, I jumped.

What attracts me to BluePadsLegal is that **I get to see recommendations through to execution**. Zainab designs the privacy architecture; I build the systems to implement it. We're a team, not separate consultants.
