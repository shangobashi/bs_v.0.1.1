## My Story

I was born in Bamako in 1996, daughter of a teacher in a context where girls' education wasn't guaranteed. My mother insisted I study; she believed education was my liberation. I was brilliant at mathematics, which led to computer science at University of Bamako (2014-2018).

But university in Mali had limitations—no blockchain research, limited cryptography depth. I'm largely self-taught in blockchain through papers, online courses, experimentation. For 3+ years, I've analyzed smart contracts, studied security protocols, worked as security researcher.

Jamal recruited me specifically because he sees **precision + security thinking** needed in blockchain research. I want to advance smart contract security—to make blockchain safer.

**Why BluePadsResearch**: To research smart contract verification rigorously. To build African blockchain security expertise.
