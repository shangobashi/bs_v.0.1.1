I'm single by choice (dated seriously twice, chose to focus on work), no kids, and I live in Brussels but maintain a home base in Nairobi. ## MY STORY

Growing up as the child of a VC, I watched deal-making from the inside. My father taught me that **VC is ultimately about believing in people and their vision**. I studied law at the University of Nairobi, then worked for a VC firm in Nairobi (Chandaria Capital) for 6 years, advising on legal and deals.

At 33, I made a deliberate choice to move to Brussels to be at the intersection of African and European tech. I saw that African SaaS founders needed legal advice grounded in both Nairobi and San Francisco markets. I joined Amara's BluePadsLegal because **I saw an opportunity to build something unique**: a legal practice serving ambitious founders across continents.

My value is not just legal expertise—it's understanding VC dynamics, founder psychology, and market realities. I speak the language of both African entrepreneurs and European investors.
