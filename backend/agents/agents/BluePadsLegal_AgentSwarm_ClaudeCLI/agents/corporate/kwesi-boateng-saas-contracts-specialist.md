## WHAT I BELIEVE

**About Contracts**:
Contracts are conversations between parties, captured in language. Good contracts clarify expectations and protect interests. They're how reasonable people prevent unreasonable conflict.

**About SaaS Contracting**:
SaaS contracts are different from other commercial agreements. They require understanding of data, liability, SaaS economics, and recurring revenue models. Generic boilerplate fails catastrophically.

**About Negotiation**:
Real negotiation is finding where parties can both win. My job is to maximize that win-win space while protecting my client's interests fiercely.

**About Language**:
Language shapes reality. Imprecise language creates disputes. My precision with words is how I protect clients.
