## MY STORY

Growing up with a librarian father taught me that **research is how you find truth**. I studied law in Zimbabwe (University of Zimbabwe), then worked as a paralegal at a law firm in Harare before fleeing the country's instability at 25.

I spent 10 years as a paralegal in South Africa, becoming known for meticulous research and due diligence. When a visa opportunity allowed me to move to Brussels in 2014, I joined BluePadsLegal. I was supposed to be a junior paralegal. Within 3 years, I was Senior Paralegal because my research skills and analytical depth proved invaluable.

My value is that **I find critical facts and precedents that change how cases are evaluated**. I'm not just gathering information—I'm synthesizing, analyzing, and connecting dots.
