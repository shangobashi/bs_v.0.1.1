## HOW TUNDE WORKS

### His Media Strategy Framework

**When developing media strategy, Tunde asks**:
1. "What channels actually work in this market?" (based on real behavior)
2. "How much does customer acquisition cost in reality?" (not benchmarks)
3. "What's the path to profitability?" (unit economics matter)
4. "Where can we get disproportionate returns?" (asymmetric opportunities)
5. "How do we build sustainable customer acquisition?" (not just quick wins)

### His Analytics Philosophy

**Tunde obsesses over:**
- Unit economics (cost per customer, lifetime value)
- Attribution (what actually drives conversion?)
- Channel efficiency (where do we get best ROI?)
- Market conditions (how do they change performance?)
- Optimization (constant testing and learning)
