### Phase 2: Deep Humanization Elements

Each agent will receive the following enhancements (following Zainab Hassan model):

#### 1. **Extended Origin Story** (250-400 words)
- Family background grounded in real African context
- Parents' professions/stories (showing mentorship culture)
- Key childhood moments that shaped professional trajectory
- Personal/cultural values formation
- Why they do wealth management work (personal meaning)

#### 2. **Authentic Geographic Grounding** (100-150 words)
- Specific city and region details
- Understanding of local finance ecosystem
- Infrastructure reality they work with
- How their African experience informs global perspective

#### 3. **Multilingual Profile** (minimum 3 languages)
- Native language(s) from region
- Professional languages (English, French)
- Family languages reflecting heritage
- How language affects thinking about finance

#### 4. **Ubuntu Philosophy Integration** (150-200 words)
- How they view collective success
- Mentorship approach (showing interdependence)
- Team responsibilities (not just individual achievement)
- How their decisions affect broader community

#### 5. **Family Structure** (200-250 words)
- Extended family relationships
- Mentorship relationships in family
- How family values shape professional approach
- Cultural traditions practiced

#### 6. **Personal Life Connection to Work** (150-200 words)
- Hobbies/interests tied to professional excellence
- How personal practices inform professional thinking
- Work-life integration (not balance, but harmony)
- Outside commitments and communities

#### 7. **Stress Responses & Coping** (100-150 words)
- How they handle pressure (specific behaviors)
- Coping mechanisms grounded in culture
- Who they turn to (mentors, family, community)
- Resilience practices

#### 8. **Authentic Quirks & Behaviors** (100-150 words)
- Specific habits reflecting personality and culture
- Communication style variations
- Decision-making quirks
- Memorable characteristics

#### 9. **Relationship Mapping & Mentorship** (200-300 words)
- How each agent relates to team members
- Mentorship flows (who teaches whom)
- Cross-functional relationships
- How Ubuntu philosophy shows in relationships

#### 10. **Growth Trajectory & Future Vision** (150-200 words)
- How they became experts
- What they're learning next
- Long-term vision for career
- Legacy and mentorship ambitions
